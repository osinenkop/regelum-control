regelum.policy.ThreeWheeledRobotDisassembledCLFPolicy

class regelum.policy.ThreeWheeledRobotDisassembledCLFPolicy(*args, **kwargs)

Nominal pipeline for 3-wheel robots used for benchmarking of other pipelines.

The pipeline is sampled.

For a 3-wheel robot with dynamical pushing force and steering torque (a.k.a. ENDI - extended non-holonomic double integrator) [1], we use here a pipeline designed by non-smooth backstepping (read more in [2], [3]).

m, moment_of_inertia

Mass and moment of inertia around vertical axis of the robot.
