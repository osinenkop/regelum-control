\(\policy \)

Policy, a law that generates actions from observations

\(\state , \State \)

State, as definite, resp., random value

\(\action , \Action \)

Action, as definite, resp., random value

\(\obs , \Obs \)

Observation, as definite, resp., random value

\(\states \)

State space

\(\actions \)

Action space

\(\obses \)

Observation space

\(\policies \)

Policy space

\(\transit \)

State dynamics law (function or probability distribution)

\(\Value ^\policy \)

Total objective (value or cost) of policy \(\policy \)

\(\Value ^*\)

Optimum total objective (value or cost) under the optimal policy \(\policy ^*\)

\(\Advan ^{\policy ,\policy '}\)

Advantage of policy \(\policy \) relative to policy \(\policy '\)

\(\policy ^\theta \)

Actor network with weights \(\theta \)

\(\hat \Value ^w\)

Critic network (state-valued) with weights \(w\)
